About Us

Our Web Application makes use of supervised learning to :

  • Detect whether a person is wearing their mask properly in real time
  • Determine the type of mask worn (surgical or N95)

We believe our Web-app will help frontline workers to ensure their safety in the face of the pandemic. Since the onset of the COVID-19 doctors, nurses, policemen and soldiers have been exposed to the risk of getting infected due to the negligence or ignorance on the part of the citizens when it comes to carrying out proper SOPs and wearing masks.
